About Me

I am a passionate PhD student at the University of Cassino and Southern Lazio, dedicated to exploring the dynamic intersection of digital transformation and sustainability. My doctoral project, in partnership with Leonardo S.p.a., focuses on developing cutting-edge frameworks that help organizations navigate the complexities of modern technological and sustainability challenges.

Current Focus

Investigating how digital transformation can drive sustainable practices in complex products and systems (CoPS).
